## 修复
- 始终允许审批时，Island 现在会先短暂延迟审批通知，并在展示前重新检查审批是否已经被免批通过，避免通知频繁弹出又马上消失。
- docked Notch 和 detached 宠物气泡共用同一套延迟逻辑，审批仍未解决时会在延迟后正常提醒。

## 说明
- Sparkle build 号提升到 `61`，版本号提升到 `0.24.1`。
- 本次发布基于 `v0.24.0` 之后的 main，重点优化始终允许后的审批通知体验。

## Fixes
- Always-allow approval prompts now wait briefly and re-check live state before opening a notification, avoiding approval popups that appear and disappear immediately after auto-approval.
- The docked Notch and detached pet bubble share the same delayed attention behavior, while unresolved approvals still surface normally after the grace window.

## Notes
- The Sparkle build number is now `61`, and the app version is now `0.24.1`.
- This release is based on latest `main` after `v0.24.0` and focuses on approval notification polish for always-allow sessions.
